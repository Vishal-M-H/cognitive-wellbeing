Asanas for mental wellbeing

Balasana

(Child's Pose)

Balasana

Calms the brain, relieves tension in the back, shoulders, and neck.

Adho Mukha Svanasana

(Downward-Facing Dog Pose)

Adho Mukha Svanasana

Increases blood flow to the brain, promoting relaxation and clarity. Helps alleviate fatigue and mild depression.

Paschimottanasana

(Seated Forward Bend)

Paschimottanasana

Relieves stress, stretches the spine, and calms the mind.

Setu Bandhasana

(Bridge Pose)

Setu Bandhasana

Opens the chest, reduces fatigue, and calms the mind.

Sukhasana

(Easy Pose)

Sukhasana

Promotes a calm mind and reduces stress.

Encourages proper breathing and mindfulness.

Nadi Shodhana

(Alternate Nostril Breathing)

Nadi Shodhana

Enhances focus and mental clarity by harmonizing brain activity.

Scientifically proven to lower cortisol levels (stress hormones).
